# History

- 1927: Founded by Mada Kokei and Ono Mitsuhiro.
- 1953: Women's Junior College opened.
- 1966: Rissho Women's University opened.
- 1969: Education Faculty established.
- 1976: Renamed Bunkyo University.
- 1977: Became coeducational.
- 1985: Information Faculty moved to Shonan.
- 1993: Graduate school established.
- 2021: Tokyo Adachi Campus opened.
- 2025: Information Society renamed Data Science.
